Hi! My name is Ruben, and love to build Solutions

Who I am?

Graduated in Business Administration, but always involved in systems and development. I have always liked to look for solutions to structural problems, through the automation of processes and procedures.

Throughout my career as an Administrator, I have come to realize that business is not more efficient, due to the lack of the right tools.

What do I like to create? I feel very comfortable creating user-oriented solutions. Systems that help create short paths. As a user, you can have all the necessary tools when making critical decisions.

I like to create environments that help, but not delay, in a learning process.

I like to learn new programming languages, but always in my heart will have a special place Javascript and PHP.

In addition to the systems, I am passionate about music and photography. In my country, I studied three years of professional photography.

The photography gave me the opportunity to create a profitable business with laboratories and photographic stores. With the opening of the era of digital cameras, a world of post-production possibilities opened up. This prompted me to also study in editing systems, such as Photoshop.
